Our Services

THE CAR MASTER provides workshop services designed to keep your vehicle dependable, safe, and ready for everyday use.

Car Repair & Maintenance

Comprehensive routine and corrective maintenance to improve reliability, reduce breakdown risk, and protect overall vehicle condition.

  • Periodic service and multi-point condition checks
  • Brake, suspension, and running repair support
  • Fluid replacement and preventive maintenance planning

Engine Diagnostics

Structured fault analysis to identify engine concerns early and reduce unnecessary repeat repairs.

  • Engine warning light and performance troubleshooting
  • System-level inspection and issue confirmation
  • Clear repair recommendations before execution

Accident Repair & Insurance Support

Coordinated post-accident repair handling with practical insurance process support to reduce customer stress.

  • Vehicle damage assessment and restoration planning
  • Repair coordination with quality-focused workflow
  • Assistance for insurance documentation and follow-up

Car Accessories

Accessory fitment and upgrades selected for durability, comfort, and utility without compromising vehicle function.

  • Interior utility and comfort accessory installation
  • Exterior add-ons with secure fitment checks
  • Guidance on practical accessory combinations
